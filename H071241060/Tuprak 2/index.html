Stardew Valley is an open-ended country-life RPG! You’ve inherited your grandfather’s old farm plot in Stardew Valley. Armed with hand-me-down tools and a few coins, you set out to begin your new life. Can you learn to live off the land and turn these overgrown fields into a thriving home? It won’t be easy. Ever since Joja Corporation came to town, the old ways of life have all but disappeared. The community center, once the town’s most vibrant hub of activity, now lies in shambles. But the valley seems full of opportunity.

Stardew Valley was created by game developer Eric Barone, under the alias of ConcernedApe. During the four years he spent solo-developing it, he taught himself the necessary skills to produce the game’s music, art, programming, and design. The game was released on PC on February 26, 2016, and within two months passed 1 million copies sold.

Create the farm of your dreams:

Turn your overgrown fields into a lively and bountiful farm! Design your layout, plant diverse crops, raise happy animals, and transform your land into a thriving agricultural paradise.

Learn to live off the land:

Raise animals, go fishing, tend to crops, craft items, or do it all! The choice is yours. Master various skills like farming, mining, foraging, and combat to truly thrive in the valley.

Become a part of the local community:

Pelican Town is home to over 30 residents you can befriend! Attend festivals, complete quests, and help restore the Community Center to bring the town back to life.

Meet someone special!:

With 12 townsfolk to date, you may even find someone to start a family with! Build relationships, get married, and experience a unique family life on your farm.

Explore vast, mysterious caves:

Encounter dangerous monsters & valuable treasures deep underground! Delve into the mines to gather precious ores and gems, but be prepared for combat.

Customize:

There are hundreds of character & home decoration options to choose from! Personalize your farmer's appearance and design your farmhouse interior to reflect your unique style.
